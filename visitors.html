Visitor Information

Getting to SFU Physics Department (In Three Easy Steps)

1. Fly to Vancouver International Airport (YVR)

2. Climb the Burnaby Mountain

Click on the map for directions.

3. Find the Physics Department

Homotopy group of our building is quite large, and can be calculated from detailed campus map.

We are located in the Shrum Science P Building (SC-P), on Level 8000, near the Physics Office (P8429).

Accomodations

Accomodations are available on campus in the Simon Hotel.

If you prefer to stay in Downtown Vancouver, we recommend the Sylvia Hotel.

Public transit in Vancouver is quite fast, and could be an alternative to car rental.
